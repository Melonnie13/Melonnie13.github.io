About Me

Hi, all, I am a hairstylist/nonprofit executive director turned Fullstack Software Engineer. While I still have a passion for service and teaching, I absolutely adore coding. When I wrote my first line of code at the age of 8, I didn't even know it was called coding; I just knew that I was building a pizza delivery game on this new thing called a computer. In college, I peeked at a friend of mine's coding homework and thought, "Oh! I know how to do that!" Fast forward to 2020 and covid, and that interest in coding still lingered at the back of my mind, so I started researching and found App Academy. From the first day at App Academy, when I realized that coding is solving logic problems, I fell head over heels in love with programming. In my spare time, I love to read and write. I am a published author and sit on the Board of Directors of Carnegie Writers, Inc. I am also an amateur animal trainer and rescue and rehabilitate neglected and abused animals of all kinds.
